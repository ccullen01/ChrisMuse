隐私政策

最后更新：2026 年 6 月 16 日

简介

无聊克星（以下简称"本应用"）尊重并保护所有使用者的个人隐私权。本隐私政策说明本应用如何收集、使用、存储和保护您的信息。

简单来说：我们不收集任何信息。

1. 信息收集

本应用不收集任何个人信息，不收集使用数据，不进行任何形式的行为追踪。

  • 不收集姓名、邮箱、手机号等个人身份信息
  • 不收集使用记录、点击数据等行为数据
  • 不收集设备信息、位置信息、通讯录
  • 不集成任何第三方分析或广告 SDK

2. 本地存储

您创建的所有习惯数据（包括习惯名称、备注、完成次数、跳过次数等）仅存储在您的设备本地，保存在 iOS 的 UserDefaults 中。

这些数据不会上传到任何服务器，不会与任何第三方共享。当您删除本应用时，这些数据会随之删除。

3. 通知权限

本应用会请求您的通知权限，用于在您设定的习惯时间前 20 分钟发送本地提醒。

所有通知都在您的设备本地生成和发送，不经过任何服务器。您可以随时在系统设置中关闭通知权限。

4. 儿童隐私

本应用不针对 14 岁以下的儿童。我们不会故意收集 14 岁以下儿童的任何信息。

5. 政策变更

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。政策变更时，我们会修改页面顶部的"最后更新"日期。重大变更会通过应用内通知告知。

6. 联系我们

如果您对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请通过以下方式联系我们：

邮箱：support@chrismuse.app

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

Introduction

ChrisMuse (the "App") respects and protects the privacy of all users. This Privacy Policy explains how the App collects, uses, stores, and protects your information.

In short: we don't collect any information.

1. Information Collection

The App does not collect any personal information, usage data, or behavioral tracking of any kind.

  • No personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
  • No usage records or click data
  • No device info, location data, or contacts
  • No third-party analytics or advertising SDKs

2. Local Storage

All habit data you create (including habit names, notes, completion counts, skip counts) is stored only on your device locally, in iOS UserDefaults.

This data is never uploaded to any server and never shared with any third party. When you delete the App, this data is deleted with it.

3. Notification Permission

The App requests notification permission to send local reminders 20 minutes before your scheduled habit time.

All notifications are generated and sent locally on your device, not through any server. You can disable notification permission at any time in system settings.

4. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 14 years of age. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 14.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When changes are made, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top. Material changes will be communicated through in-app notifications.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@chrismuse.app
